<p>Every Singaporean household will receive $300 CDC Vouchers in January
2025.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The CDC Vouchers Scheme 2025 (January) is part of the enhancements to
the</p>
<p>Assurance Package announced by then Deputy Prime Minister and Minister</p>
<p>for Finance Lawrence Wong at Budget 2024. The package provides more</p>
<p>immediate support for Singaporean households and helps defray daily expenses.</p>
<p>Starting 3 January 2025, you can visit &lt;a target="redeemsg" href="
<a href="https://go.gov.sg/cdcv&quot;><strong>go.gov.sg/cdcv</strong></a>" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/cdcv"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;go.gov.sg/cdcv&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/a&gt;</a>to
claim the $300 CDC vouchers. The process for claiming and spending the
vouchers</p>
<p>remains the same.</p>
<p>The CDC Vouchers Scheme 2025 (January) are valid till 31 December 2024</p>
<p><a href="/residents/info" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Learn</a> how
you can claim your CDC Vouchers 2025 (January).</p>
